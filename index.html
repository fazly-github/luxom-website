About Luxom

Why Mobile Detailing Beats the Car Wash

A traditional car wash is designed for speed and volume — not quality. Automated machines use the same brushes on hundreds of cars and can't reach corners, door jambs, or interior crevices. Our mobile detailers spend dedicated time on your specific vehicle, using professional-grade tools that car washes simply don't carry.

The Luxom Difference

We started Luxom because we couldn't find a detailer that truly respected your time and your vehicle. We come to you, work around your schedule, and treat every car with the same care we'd want for our own.

Our promise: Every detail is performed by a trained professional with premium products — or we come back and make it right, no questions asked.

Before We Arrive

Service
Requirements

To ensure we deliver the best possible result — and that your appointment goes smoothly — please review the following requirements before booking. These help us protect your vehicle and keep our process efficient for everyone.

🚗

Vehicle Access

  • Vehicle must be accessible and unlocked at the scheduled time
  • Keys available for interior services requiring door/trunk access
  • Windows should be fully closed prior to our arrival
  • Remove personal belongings and loose items from interior
  • Car seats must be removed by owner before interior detailing
📍

Location & Space

  • Flat, paved surface required (driveway, parking lot, or apron)
  • Access to a standard outdoor water spigot for exterior washes
  • At least 10–12 feet of clear space around the vehicle
  • Covered or shaded areas preferred but not required
  • Notify us of gate codes or entry restrictions before arrival
📅

Scheduling & Cancellation

  • Appointments confirmed at least 24 hours in advance
  • Cancellations require minimum 12 hours notice
  • Extreme weather reschedules are offered at no charge
  • 15-minute grace period; late arrivals may require rescheduling
  • Same-day bookings subject to availability — contact us directly
🔍

Vehicle Condition

  • Heavily soiled vehicles may require an additional fee — we notify you first
  • Pre-existing paint damage is noted before service begins
  • We do not detail vehicles with mold, biohazard, or sewage issues
  • Pet hair removal available for additional charge — request at booking
  • Engine bay cleaning requires engine to be cool before service
💳

Payment

  • Payment is due upon service completion
  • We accept cash, Zelle, Venmo, CashApp & major cards
  • Final invoice provided after every service
  • Custom packages and bundle pricing available — ask during your quote
  • Gratuity is not required but always appreciated
🛡️

Health & Safety

  • We use professional-grade, vehicle-safe products on all surfaces
  • Keep children and pets away from work area during service
  • We are fully insured — any concerns addressed professionally
  • All technicians follow local regulations and safety guidelines
  • Disclose any product sensitivities or allergies at booking
💬
