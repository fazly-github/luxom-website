Protect Your Car's Paint with Luxom Auto Detailing

Welcome to Luxom Auto Detailing — where we bring the detail shop directly to you across McKinney and the entire DFW Metroplex. We specialize in premium mobile detailing services designed to protect your vehicle's paint, restore your interior, and keep every surface looking its absolute best.

Our process goes far beyond a standard car wash. We use professional-grade, pH-neutral shampoos, real microfiber wash mitts, clay bars, steam cleaners, and specialized interior tools to treat every surface with precision and care — whether that's paint, leather, glass, plastic, rubber, or chrome.

Premium Mobile Detailing in McKinney, TX

Our team of experienced detailers will work with you to identify the right service for your vehicle's specific needs. We use only the highest quality products on the market, and our mobile process is designed to be as efficient as possible — so you can get back on the road looking great without any disruption to your day.

Did you know? Vehicles that receive regular professional detailing retain significantly higher resale values compared to neglected vehicles. A car with a flawless, protected exterior is sure to attract top dollar when it's time to sell.

Expert Detailing — Right at Your Door

At Luxom, we understand that your vehicle is more than just transportation — it's an investment. That's why we treat every car as if it were our own, ensuring that every service is performed with care, precision, and pride in our work. Whether you're protecting a brand-new vehicle or restoring an older one, we have the tools and expertise to deliver real results.

Protect Your Investment

Our mobile detailing services are designed to give you real peace of mind on the road. With our signature treatments applied, you can drive confidently knowing your paint is protected from UV damage, environmental contaminants, bird droppings, and everyday road wear.
