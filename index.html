Before We Arrive

Service
Requirements

Please review before booking so your appointment goes smoothly and we can deliver the best possible result for your vehicle.

🚗

Vehicle Access

  • Vehicle accessible & unlocked at appointment time
  • Keys available for interior services
  • Windows fully closed before arrival
  • Personal items & valuables removed
  • Car seats removed by owner prior to interior detail
📍

Location & Space

  • Flat paved surface — driveway, lot, or apron
  • Outdoor water spigot access for exterior washes
  • 10–12 ft clear space around vehicle
  • Shade preferred but not required
  • Notify us of gate codes before arrival
📅

Scheduling

  • Confirm appointments 24 hours in advance
  • Cancellations require 12 hours notice
  • Weather reschedules offered at no charge
  • 15-minute grace period on arrival
  • Same-day bookings subject to availability
🔍

Vehicle Condition

  • Heavy soiling may incur additional fee — notified first
  • Pre-existing damage noted before we begin
  • No mold, biohazard, or sewage situations
  • Pet hair removal available — request at booking
  • Engine must be cool before bay cleaning
💳

Payment

  • Payment due upon service completion
  • Cash, Zelle, Venmo, CashApp & major cards accepted
  • Invoice provided after every service
  • Bundle pricing available — ask during quote
  • Gratuity not required but appreciated
🛡️

Health & Safety

  • Professional-grade, vehicle-safe products used
  • Keep children & pets away during service
  • Fully insured — concerns addressed professionally
  • All technicians follow local safety guidelines
  • Disclose product allergies at time of booking
